A customer submits a support ticket.

Or they can ring in to the call centre.

Call centre staff take the ticket and process it. 
This involves sending it to engineers. 
When the issue is resolved the engineers email the call centre staff
who contact the customer with the resolution, by email, SMS, or phone.
